Since 2013 Novara Media has emerged as a crucial voice on the UK left, reaching major audiences with its podcasts, videos and articles. And all for free.

We have a vision to transform British media, and politics, between now and 2020, emphasising issues the mainstream too frequently ignores: work, race, gender, climate change and austerity.

And in the last year we’ve made real progress. We launched our subscriber network in late 2015, and since then we’ve been able to pay our contributors for the first time, move in to a permanent studio, buy equipment to scale our video operation and organise regular events.

But thats only the start. Over the next year we will be publishing over 250 original essays while creating daily podcast and video content. Furthermore, we’ll be hosting events for subscribers in London and national events for anyone who cares to come along.

The scale of the task ahead can not be understated. Creating a new media for a different politics in these difficult times requires hard work. Even more importantly it needs your support.

By making a donation to Novara Media, or an ongoing subscription, you help us take our work to the next level, finding new, larger audiences and shaping mainstream debate for the better. We have to champion the unrepresented and the unheard, nobody will do it for us.
